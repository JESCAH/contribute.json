The Nitty Gritty

We use json-schema.org as a standard for the JSON you can define.

The format takes some getting used to but it's really simple actually.

What to look out for are the keys that are mentioned under "required". For example, on the top-level the required keys are: name, description and repository.

When in doubt, perhaps look at some of the existing Examples.
